# International Payments Learning Skill

Use this skill when the user wants to build, expand, or maintain an Obsidian-based learning system for international payments.

## Core Goal

Turn payments knowledge into a durable study system that supports:

- business-first learning
- Chinese-first notes with key English terms retained
- expert capability maps
- practical cases
- dashboard and reporting views
- progress tracking and interruption recovery
- business and technical expert tracks
- team onboarding and role-based learning paths
- market-case learning for region-specific judgment
- country deep dives for priority markets
- scenario playbooks for concrete business models
- combo cases for country x scenario decisions

## Workflow

1. Start from the business problems, not infrastructure history.
2. Build or update the domain `README.md`, `主题索引.md`, `学习进度.md`, and `恢复笔记.md`.
3. When the domain is mature enough, add role-based onboarding pages for:
   - senior payments expert
   - payments business expert
   - payments technical expert
4. Add market-case pages when the domain needs region-specific judgment.
5. Add country deep dives when the learner is preparing to prioritize specific markets.
6. Add scenario playbooks when the domain needs business-model-specific guidance.
7. Add combo cases when the learner needs to reason about a concrete country and business model at the same time.
8. Organize payment knowledge into these major areas:
   - expert frameworks and learning tracks
   - business results and operating metrics
   - transaction mechanisms
   - operations and governance
   - technical architecture and consistency
   - market cases, localization, and country judgment
   - scenario playbooks and operating patterns
   - combo cases and operating tradeoffs
   - background rules and rails
9. For core topics, prefer note structure in this order:
   - what problem this topic solves
   - core model or mechanism
   - practical actions
   - business case
   - common mistakes
   - related notes
10. Add Mermaid maps when the topic benefits from a system view.
11. When the topic is operational, add dashboard, report, or checklist guidance.
12. Before ending a study session, update progress and resume notes.

## Writing Rules

- Default to Chinese for explanations.
- Keep key English terms inline when they are industry-standard, such as `3DS`, `PSP`, `Acquirer`, `Chargeback`, `BIN`, `Webhook`, `PCI DSS`, `UPI`, `Pix`.
- Optimize for practical business understanding, not textbook completeness.
- Prefer business cases that show tradeoffs between success rate, risk, cost, and stability.
- Include separate learning tracks when useful: senior payments expert, payments business expert, and payments technical expert.
- Use market cases to connect general rules to region-specific behavior.
- Use country deep dives to sharpen market prioritization.
- Use scenario playbooks to connect the same payment system to different business models.
- Use combo cases to show how country differences and business-model differences collide in real decisions.
- When possible, pair each scenario with an operating template or dashboard.
